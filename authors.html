Paper submission

All the papers should be submitted by sending the manuscript to tongjidai@tongji.edu.cn.

Papers will be peer-reviewed by the experts.

According to the review comments, the paper will be accepted, required to revise or rejected.

Paper publication

All accepted papers will be presented orally or by post in the symposium.

Selected papers will be recommended to well-known SCI journals.
